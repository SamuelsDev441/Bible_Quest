The Bible Quest project will be multi-step, split into 7 parts:
The Beginning and Creation
The Promise
God's People
The Kingdom
Exile and Hope
The Savior
The Church


Level 1 Prototype: “The Garden of Beginnings”

Objective: Help Adam and Eve care for the Garden and learn to trust God.
Gameplay Mix:

Explore Eden, collect fruit, name animals (mini-game)

Puzzle: Sort fruit that’s allowed vs forbidden

Trivia: “On which day did God create man?”

Story Choice: Obey or disobey God (leads to lesson scene)

Verse Unlock: Genesis 1:31 – “And God saw everything that He had made…”

Rewards:

Unlock “Faith Gem”

Progress to “Noah’s Ark” level
